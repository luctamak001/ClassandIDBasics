Baked Salmon Bite Size

Ingredients

  • ½ cup oil for frying (essential)
  • 8 ounces wild Pacific salmon fillets, cut into 1-inch cubes (essential)
  • ½ teaspoon salt (essential)
  • ½ teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch

Instructions

  1. Combine cayenne, turmeric, and salt in a bowl; add salmon and toss to coat. Set aside for 15 minutes. (important)
  2. Add salmon to the skillet; cook until golden brown, about 1 minute per side. (important)
  3. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. While oil is heating, sprinkle cornstarch over salmon; toss to coat.
  4. Cook Time: 5 mins
  5. Additional Time: 15 mins
